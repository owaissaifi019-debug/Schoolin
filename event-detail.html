National Science & Robotics Fest 2026

Hosted by: 🏫 Delhi Public School, RK Puram

About the Event

The National Science & Robotics Fest is India's premier high school technology event, designed to bring together student innovators, coders, and makers to display robotics projects and science models.

This year's event features autonomous line-following competitions, science exhibitions, and coding hackathons. Participating schools get direct feedback from judges from leading technology institutes and research fests.

Guidelines & Eligibility

  • Open to students of Grades IX to XII from any affiliated board across India.
  • A school can register up to 3 teams per category.
  • Team sizes must consist of a minimum of 2 and a maximum of 4 members.
  • All model designs must be drafted and built by students; pre-fabricated commercial assembly kits are restricted.
