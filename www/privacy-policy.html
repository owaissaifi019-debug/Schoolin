Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. Read how CampusLink handles, secures, and protects your information.

Introduction

Welcome to CampusLink ("we," "our," "us"). We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and what rights you have in relation to it when you use our school networking platform (located at index.html) and related services.

Information We Collect

We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when registering on the platform, expressing an interest in obtaining information about us or our products, participating in activities, or otherwise contacting us.

  • Personal Details: Name, email address, password, profile photo, and user type (student, teacher, parent, alumni, or school representative).
  • Academic and School Profiles: Affiliated school name, location, board affiliation, class/grade, achievements, sports, and certificates.
  • Communication Data: Message history, connection requests, and inquiry forms submitted through the platform.

How We Use Your Information

We process your information for purposes based on legitimate business interests, the fulfillment of our contract with you, compliance with our legal obligations, and/or your consent. Specifically, we use it to:

  • Facilitate account creation and logon process.
  • Build and manage your professional school network profiles.
  • Enable features like event sharing, student admissions tracking, and inter-school connectivity.
  • Deliver relevant notifications, messages, and security alerts.

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We only share information with your consent, to comply with laws, to provide you with services, to protect your rights, or to fulfill business obligations. Your academic and school profiles are publicly viewable by other authenticated members of the CampusLink platform to facilitate networking. We do not sell or lease your personal information to third-party marketing services.

Data Security and Protection

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures designed to protect the security of any personal information we process. All authentication and database operations are managed through secure endpoints provided by Supabase with Row Level Security (RLS) policies enabled. However, please also remember that we cannot guarantee that the internet itself is 100% secure.

Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights under applicable data protection laws. These may include the right to request access and obtain a copy of your personal information, to request rectification or erasure, and to restrict the processing of your personal information. You can update your profile details anytime via your profile settings page.

Contact Us

If you have questions or comments about this policy, you may email us at supportatcampuslink@gmail.com or contact our support representative team.
